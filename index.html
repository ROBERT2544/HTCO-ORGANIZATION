Background History

It begun as a group WASH THE TOILET, we had an ambition to see the ways we can help a poor child in school to learn in an hygienic environment mostly in toilet/ latrine sector of the schools in the schools around Nairobi County. this was in the early 2019 before the spike of Covid-19 virus.
